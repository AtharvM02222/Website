NYSC

Regional Round

Initially built using Arduino, HC-05 Bluetooth Module, L298 Motor Driver, and 250 RPM DC Motors. Later upgraded to an ESP32 board for better control and flexibility. Our team qualified for nationals with a solid score of 3 – 0.

National Round

For the nationals, I designed two advanced bots using ESP32: a Defender with 300 RPM Johnson Motor and an Attacker with 500 RPM Johnson Motor. The setup used BTS Motor Drivers and Bluetooth Control. We won with scores of 6 – 3 and 6 – 0, and proudly reached the quarter-finals.
