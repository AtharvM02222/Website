NYSC Project

Regional Round

  • Initially used Arduino, HC-05, and L298 motor driver with 250 RPM DC motors.
  • Later upgraded to ESP32 for improved performance.
  • Qualified for nationals with a score of 3–0.

National Round

  • Built two bots using ESP32 and Bluetooth control.
  • Defender used a 300 RPM Johnson motor, Attacker had a 500 RPM Johnson motor.
  • Controlled using BTS motor driver. Match scores: 6–3, 6–0, lost in quarter-finals.
